# privacy-policy
Privacy Policy for Waypoint: Goal Planner.

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Information We Collect

Waypoint collects minimal data to provide you with the best experience. This includes your profile name, goals, waypoints, and progress data — all stored locally on your device. If you subscribe to Waypoint Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple and we do not have access to your payment information. How We Use Your Data

Your goal and progress data is used solely to power your personal roadmaps and track your achievements. When you use AI-generated roadmaps, your goal inputs are sent to our third-party AI provider (Rork AI Toolkit) to generate personalized waypoints. See the “AI-Generated Content & Third-Party AI Service” section below for full details. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

All of your goals, waypoints, tasks, and profile information are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not maintain server-side copies of your personal data. Deleting the app will permanently remove all locally stored data.

AI-Generated Content & Third-Party AI Service

Waypoint uses a third-party AI service — Rork AI Toolkit (hosted at toolkit.rork.com) — to generate personalized goal roadmaps.

What we send: When you tap “Generate Roadmap,” the app sends only the following user-provided inputs to the AI provider: • Goal title • Goal description • Goal category • Timeline

What we do NOT send: No personal identifiers, account information, device data, location, or any other sensitive information is transmitted to the AI provider.

Purpose: This data is used solely to generate a personalized roadmap of waypoints and tasks for your goal. It is not stored by the AI provider or used for any other purpose.

Content moderation is performed entirely on-device before any data is sent. After generation, you can review the full AI-generated roadmap before choosing to save it. Subscriptions

Waypoint Pro subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Subscription status is verified through RevenueCat, a third-party service that communicates with Apple’s servers. Please refer to Apple’s privacy policy for information about how Apple handles your purchase data.

Analytics & Tracking

Waypoint does not use third-party analytics or advertising frameworks. We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Children’s Privacy

Waypoint is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app. Continued use of Waypoint after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please reach out to us through the App Store listing.
